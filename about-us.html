About Us

Who We Are

Yogit Global Import Export LLP is a fresh and passionate import-export firm based in India. We specialize in sourcing and delivering premium agricultural products — such as rice, spices, and other commodities — to international markets.

As a new business, we are committed to ethical sourcing, transparent operations, and building lasting global relationships. Every product we deliver reflects our dedication to quality and consistency.

Our Mission

To connect Indian farmers and producers with the world by offering premium quality products and seamless export services.

Our Vision

To become a globally respected and trusted name in agricultural exports, known for transparency, integrity, and quality.

Our Values

Trust, Quality, Responsibility, Innovation, and Sustainability.

Why Choose Yogit Global?

  • Transparent Business Practices
  • Strong supplier relationships across India
  • Global export compliance and documentation
  • Fresh and authentic Indian products
